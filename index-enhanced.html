FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our appraisal services.

How long does a typical appraisal take?

We provide efficient appraisal services with turnaround times varying based on property complexity and scope. Complex or specialized properties may take longer, but we'll provide a clear timeline upfront.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Southwestern Ontario, with particular expertise in London, Windsor, Guelph, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Are your appraisals accepted by all lenders?

Yes, our AACI-designated appraisers are recognized by all major Canadian lenders, government agencies, and courts. Our reports meet all industry standards and regulatory requirements.

What information do you need to get started?

We'll need the property address, intended use of the appraisal, your timeline, and any relevant property details. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements during your initial consultation.
