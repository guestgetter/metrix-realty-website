Frequently Asked Questions

Metrix Realty has provided appraisal and advisory services to municipal, provincial, and federal government clients for over 30 years. Our experience includes expropriation valuations, surplus property disposition, asset management, policy development support, and testimony before the Ontario Land Tribunal.

Under Ontario's Expropriations Act, we assess the market value of lands being acquired, Injurious Affection (loss in value to remaining lands not taken), and Disturbance Damages (reasonable costs associated with relocation and disruption). Our valuations meet all statutory requirements and can be defended before the Ontario Land Tribunal.

Injurious Affection refers to the loss in value to remaining lands that are not expropriated but are adversely affected by the expropriation or the public works being constructed. Under Ontario's Expropriations Act, property owners may be entitled to compensation for Injurious Affection in addition to the market value of lands taken.
