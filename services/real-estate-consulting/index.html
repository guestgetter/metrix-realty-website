Consulting & Advisory Services

Our Services

Our consulting practice provides valuable insights for developers, investors, and property owners through comprehensive market analysis, feasibility studies, and strategic advisory.

Market Analysis & Research

Comprehensive market studies providing detailed analysis of local and regional real estate trends, comparable sales, rental market conditions, absorption rates, and supply and demand dynamics.

Feasibility Studies

Professional assessment of development projects evaluating financial viability, market potential, construction costs, absorption timelines, and risk factors for informed go/no-go decisions.

Investment Analysis

Strategic investment advisory including portfolio analysis, cash flow projections, DCF modeling, cap rate analysis, and return on investment calculations for real estate ventures.

Highest & Best Use Studies

Analysis determining the most profitable and legally permissible use of a property, considering zoning, market demand, physical characteristics, and financial viability to maximize value.

Portfolio Valuations

Comprehensive valuation services for multi-property portfolios providing detailed analysis for institutional investors, pension funds, and property managers for financial reporting and strategic planning.

Strategic Advisory Services

Expert consultation on real estate strategy, market positioning, asset management, and long-term planning to support informed business decisions, corporate relocations, and organizational growth.

Data-Driven Analysis

Our Approach

Our consulting practice is built on comprehensive data, rigorous analytical methodologies, and three decades of local market expertise. Every recommendation is supported by verifiable evidence.

Market Trend Analysis

Tracking pricing trends, absorption rates, vacancy rates, and market cycles across Southwestern Ontario.

Financial Modeling

Cash flow projections, DCF analysis, IRR calculations, and sensitivity testing to evaluate investment returns.

Demographic & Economic Research

Population trends, employment data, income analysis, and economic indicators from Statistics Canada and CMHC.

Regulatory & Zoning Analysis

Official plan policies, zoning by-laws, development charges, and municipal approval processes.

Our Process

Consulting Process

1

Define Scope & Objectives

Understand your goals, identify key questions, and define the deliverables needed to support your decision.

2

3

Analysis & Modeling

Apply appropriate analytical frameworks, financial models, and professional judgment informed by three decades of local expertise.

4

Report & Recommendations

Deliver clear, actionable findings supported by data and professional analysis — ready for lenders, investors, or development approvals.
