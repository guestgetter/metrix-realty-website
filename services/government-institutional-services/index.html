Specialized Services

Services for Government Clients

Our advisory services are designed to support negotiations, acquisitions, dispositions, expropriation proceedings, and policy decisions with the highest standards of professional competence.

Expropriation Valuations

Fair compensation analysis under Ontario's Expropriations Act including market value of lands taken, Injurious Affection, and Disturbance Damages.

Acquisition & Disposition Valuations

Independent valuations supporting property transactions for municipalities and institutions, ensuring fair market value is achieved in all transfers.

Asset Management & Portfolio Valuations

Comprehensive valuations for municipal and institutional property holdings, providing accurate asset values for financial reporting and strategic planning.

Policy Development Support

Market analysis and valuation expertise to inform municipal planning and policy decisions, including land use, housing, and development strategy.

Surplus Property Valuation

Market value assessments for surplus government and institutional properties being offered for sale, ensuring transparent and defensible pricing.

Ontario Land Tribunal Support

Expert witness testimony and valuation support before the Ontario Land Tribunal for expropriation, planning, and municipal matters.
