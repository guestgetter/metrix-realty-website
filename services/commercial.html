Our Commercial Appraisal Process

Every commercial appraisal follows our proven methodology using all three approaches to value.

1

Cost Approach

Replacement cost new less depreciation plus land value. Particularly useful for newer properties and special purpose buildings.

2

Sales Comparison

Analysis of comparable sales with adjustments for differences in size, location, condition, and other value-affecting factors.

3

Income Approach

Capitalization of net operating income and discounted cash flow analysis for income-producing properties.
