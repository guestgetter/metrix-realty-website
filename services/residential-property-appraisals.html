When You Need Us

When Is a Residential Appraisal Required?

Whether for financing, legal proceedings, or estate matters, our designated appraisers provide reliable valuations for every situation.

Mortgage & Purchase Financing

Lenders require an independent appraisal before approving a mortgage to ensure the property's value supports the loan amount. Accepted by all major Canadian financial institutions.

Refinancing & HELOC

Updated equity valuations for refinancing or home equity line of credit applications. Determine your property's current market value to access available equity.

Estate Planning & Probate

Accurate valuations for estate settlement, calculating estate taxes, determining fair distribution among beneficiaries, and satisfying CRA reporting requirements.

Divorce & Matrimonial Property

Court-ready reports for Ontario Family Law Act proceedings. Independent valuations for both parties at the required dates — date of marriage and date of separation.

Property Tax Appeals

Professional valuations to support assessment appeals before Ontario's Assessment Review Board if you believe your MPAC assessment is inaccurate.

Relocation & Pre-Listing

Market value confirmation for corporate relocations and personal moves. Unbiased opinion of value to inform your listing strategy and sale negotiations.

Our Process

What to Expect

Consultation

2
