Our Services

Consulting & Advisory Services

Our consulting services provide valuable insights for developers, investors, and property owners through comprehensive market analysis, feasibility studies, and strategic advisory.

Market Analysis & Research

Comprehensive market studies providing detailed analysis of local and regional real estate trends, comparable sales, rental market conditions, absorption rates, and supply/demand dynamics.

Who needs this: Developers, investors, municipalities

Feasibility Studies

Professional assessment of development projects evaluating financial viability, market potential, construction costs, absorption timelines, and risk factors for informed go/no-go decisions.

Who needs this: Developers, investors, lenders

Investment Analysis

Strategic investment advisory including portfolio analysis, cash flow projections, DCF modeling, cap rate analysis, and return on investment calculations for real estate ventures.

Who needs this: Investors, pension funds, REITs

Highest & Best Use Studies

Analysis determining the most profitable and legally permissible use of a property, considering zoning, market demand, physical characteristics, and financial viability to maximize value.

Who needs this: Property owners, developers, municipalities

Portfolio Valuations

Comprehensive valuation services for multi-property portfolios, providing detailed analysis for institutional investors, pension funds, and property managers for financial reporting and strategic planning.

Who needs this: Institutional investors, asset managers

Strategic Advisory

Expert consultation on real estate strategy, market positioning, asset management, and long-term planning to support informed business decisions, corporate relocations, and organizational growth.

Who needs this: Corporations, non-profits, business owners

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

An appraisal provides a formal opinion of market value for a specific property as of a specific date. A consulting assignment provides analysis, recommendations, or conclusions on broader real estate questions such as market feasibility, investment strategy, highest and best use, or development potential. Both are completed under CUSPAP standards by accredited professionals.

Feasibility studies are typically required before committing capital to a development project. Lenders, investors, and developers use them to evaluate whether a proposed project is financially viable given market conditions, construction costs, absorption rates, and projected returns. They are also common for rezoning applications and municipal planning approvals.

Our market analysis is supported by CoStar Professional data, TRREB market intelligence, CMHC housing data, Statistics Canada demographics, municipal planning documents, and proprietary local market databases developed over three decades of practice in Southwestern Ontario.

Yes. We provide market analysis, absorption studies, and financial feasibility analysis to support development planning. Our work helps developers understand market demand, optimal product mix, pricing strategy, and project timing to maximize returns and minimize risk.

A highest and best use study determines the most profitable, legally permissible, physically possible, and financially feasible use of a property. This considers zoning regulations, market demand, physical site characteristics, and financial viability to identify the use that maximizes the property's value.
