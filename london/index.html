FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve from your London office?

From our London office at 620 Richmond St, we directly serve London, St. Thomas, Strathroy, Dorchester, Komoka, Delaware, Ilderton, Ingersoll, Woodstock, Tillsonburg, and all of Middlesex, Elgin, and Oxford counties. We also provide province-wide coverage across Ontario for all property types.

Are your London appraisers AACI designated?

Yes, our London team includes AACI (Accredited Appraiser Canadian Institute) designated professionals. This is the highest designation in Canadian real estate appraisal, recognized by lenders, courts, and institutions. Our appraisers also hold CRA and other professional designations.

Do you provide appraisals for divorce/matrimonial purposes?

Yes, we provide independent, unbiased property valuations for matrimonial and family law matters. Our appraisals are prepared to a standard that holds up in court, and our appraisers can provide expert witness testimony if required. We work with family lawyers throughout London and Southwestern Ontario.
