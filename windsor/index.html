FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve from your Windsor office?

From our Windsor office at 2557 Dougall Ave, we serve Windsor, LaSalle, Tecumseh, Amherstburg, Essex, Kingsville, Leamington, Lakeshore, and all of Essex County. We also serve Chatham-Kent and provide province-wide coverage across Ontario for commercial, industrial, and litigation appraisal needs.

Do you provide single-family residential appraisals in Windsor?

Our Windsor office specializes in commercial, industrial, multi-family, and litigation appraisals. For single-family residential appraisals in the Windsor area, we recommend contacting a local residential appraisal firm that can provide competitive pricing and faster turnaround. If you have a complex residential valuation need (e.g., estate, litigation, or multi-family), please contact us to discuss how we can help.

Do you appraise automotive and industrial properties?

Yes, we have extensive experience appraising automotive manufacturing facilities, tier-1 supplier properties, and industrial real estate throughout Windsor-Essex. Our appraisers understand the unique factors affecting industrial property values in this region, including proximity to the border, transportation infrastructure, and the evolving EV battery manufacturing sector.
