Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Property Appraisals

Get instant answers to the most common questions about our appraisal services, turnaround times, and pricing.

How quickly can you complete my property appraisal?

Most residential appraisals are completed within 24-48 hours, while commercial properties typically take 3-5 business days. We also offer same-day rush service for urgent needs at no additional charge for qualifying properties.

Are your appraisals accepted by all major banks and lenders?

Yes, our AACI-designated appraisers are approved by all major Canadian banks and financial institutions. Our reports meet or exceed all industry standards and regulatory requirements for mortgage financing, refinancing, and investment purposes.

What areas do you serve in Ontario?

We provide appraisal services throughout Southwestern Ontario, including London, Windsor, Guelph, Kitchener-Waterloo, Sarnia, Chatham-Kent, Oxford County, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

How much does a property appraisal cost?

Appraisal fees vary based on property type, size, complexity, and turnaround requirements. Residential appraisals typically start at $450, while commercial properties vary based on scope. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs.

Can you provide expert witness testimony for legal proceedings?

Absolutely. Our AACI-designated appraisers are court-qualified expert witnesses with a 100% acceptance rate in legal proceedings. We provide litigation support for matrimonial disputes, expropriation cases, insurance claims, and other legal matters requiring professional property valuation testimony.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we need the property address, type of appraisal required (residential, commercial, litigation), intended use of the report, and your preferred timeline. You can provide this information through our online form or by calling us directly at (519) 672-7550.
